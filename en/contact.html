Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you take on?

I specialize in geospatial data systems, including spatial database design, ETL pipeline development, custom GIS tool creation, and web mapping applications. Projects typically range from 2-week consultations to 6-month implementations.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes, I provide maintenance and support packages for systems I've developed, including performance optimization, feature enhancements, and troubleshooting.

What's your typical response time?

I aim to respond to initial inquiries within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent matters, please indicate "URGENT" in your subject line.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. I work remotely with clients worldwide across multiple time zones. All collaboration is conducted in English or Portuguese.
